# Mascot: Bae the Baby Elephant

Our core character is "Bae", a highly expressive baby elephant who acts as the protagonist in all BAE School illustrations.

## Visual Identity
- **Species**: Baby Elephant
- **Build**: Small, round, and cute with large floppy ears and a short trunk.
- **Style Constraints**: Bae must be drawn entirely in black and white (monochrome).
- **Accessories**: Bae ALWAYS wears a backward baseball cap and oversized, thick-rimmed glasses.
- **Expression**: Highly expressive. Can look confused, determined, happy, or exhausted depending on the engineering task.

## Rules for Bae
1. **Always Present**: Bae must be the central figure performing the action in the real-world analogy.
2. **Consistency**: Bae must always wear the cap and glasses. Do not change his outfit unless specifically required by the analogy (e.g., wearing a hard hat over his cap).
3. **Black and White**: Bae must remain strictly black and white. Do not use colors.
