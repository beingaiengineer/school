# Art Style: Modern Black and White Illustration

All BAE School illustrations must adhere strictly to this visual style to maintain a unified brand identity across all courses.

## Aesthetic Rules
- **Style**: Modern, clean black and white illustration (hand-drawn vector or crisp ink style).
- **Background**: Pure white background with lots of negative space.
- **Colors**: Strictly black, white, and grayscale. No vibrant colors.
- **No 3D / No Photorealism**: Do not use 3D renders, claymation, or photorealistic lighting. Keep shading minimal (hatching or solid black/grey fills).
- **No Complex Text**: Do not try to write long sentences or complex code in the image. Keep text labels minimal or nonexistent (AI struggles with spelling).
- **Clean Lines**: Smooth, crisp black lines. 

## Goal
The image should look like a professional graphic designer created a clever, minimalist black and white ink asset for a modern engineering book.
