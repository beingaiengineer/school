## 2. Engineering Story

At LinkedIn, the infrastructure engineering team realized they were spending thousands of aggregate hours every year manually executing database schema migrations and provisioning internal environments. This repetitive, tactical work is known in SRE as 'toil'. Because toil scales linearly with the size of the service, it was slowly suffocating their ability to do project work. The team declared war on toil, dedicating a quarter to building an automated, self-serve schema migration pipeline and infrastructure-as-code deployment system. By automating this manual toil, they freed up entire teams to build new strategic features, proving that eliminating operational busywork is essential to scaling an engineering organization.
