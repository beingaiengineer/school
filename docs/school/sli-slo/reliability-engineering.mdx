## 2. Engineering Story

E-commerce platforms like Shopify face their ultimate test during Black Friday flash sales, where traffic can spike 100x in a matter of seconds. Traditional ops teams would just provision more servers, but true reliability engineering requires systemic resilience. Months before the event, the reliability team load tests the platform at 10x the expected peak traffic, forcefully simulating database failovers, cache evictions, and third-party payment gateway timeouts. By intentionally breaking the system in staging and implementing robust retry mechanisms, circuit breakers, and degraded-mode fallbacks, they ensure that the platform remains highly available. This proactive reliability engineering directly translates to millions of dollars in saved revenue during peak events.
