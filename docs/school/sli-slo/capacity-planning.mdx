## 2. Engineering Story

Operating at a global scale requires immense foresight. Meta (Facebook) must analyze complex power, cooling, and network constraints across their global data centers to perform accurate capacity planning. It's not just about buying more servers; it's about predicting user traffic surges during massive regional events like New Year's Eve or the Super Bowl. Meta's capacity engineers built sophisticated demand-forecasting models that automatically route traffic geographically to ensure no single data center exceeds its compute or power capacity. By proactively simulating these massive traffic spikes months in advance, they ensure the infrastructure is physically and logically prepared before the load ever arrives.
