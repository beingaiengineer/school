## 3. Concept Explanation

Bro, Camel AI is built on the concept of 'role-playing' agents. You define two agents—like a Python Programmer and a Stock Trader—and let them autonomously converse and collaborate to solve a complex task. It's fascinating for simulation. It's crucial to understand when to use this versus standard scripts.

## 9. Top Interview Questions

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| What makes Camel AI unique? | Its specific approach to state and tooling. |
| When should I avoid it? | When a simple rule-based script is enough. |

## 10. Tricky Questions & Edge Cases

Bro, what happens if the LLM hallucinates a tool call? Camel AI has specific guardrails, but you must configure them properly or you'll get infinite loops.
