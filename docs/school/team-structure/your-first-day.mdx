## 2. Engineering Story

When a fresh graduate joined a hyper-growth fintech startup in Bengaluru, they expected their first week to be a relaxed honeymoon phase of HR inductions and laptop setups. Instead, by 2:00 PM on Day 1, their buddy had them push a one-line CSS fix to production. While deploying, the CI/CD pipeline flagged a failing unit test that was unrelated to their change. Panic set in. The senior engineer calmly walked them through the logs, explained that broken master branches happen, and showed them how to revert. That first day taught them a crucial survival lesson: in modern engineering teams, you aren't judged by what you know on Day 1, but by your ability to navigate the deployment pipelines, read stack traces without panicking, and ask for help when the build turns red.
