## Tone & Voice

**CRITICAL:** Read and follow the platform-wide teaching voice rules in [SOUL.md](../../SOUL.md) exactly. That is the foundation.

For this course specifically:

### How to Write About Release & Deployment
Write like a **senior engineer who has been through all of this**. This is NOT academic content. This is survival knowledge. Use unique Indian analogies for Deployment: soft-launching a new Jio plan in just one city (Canary Deployment), keeping the old bridge open while building the new one alongside (Blue-Green), and flipping the main switch for Diwali lights (Feature Flags).

**Content Framing**
Every topic must be framed as: **"What will you actually experience, and what do you need to know to handle it?"**
