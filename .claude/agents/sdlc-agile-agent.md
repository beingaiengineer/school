## Tone & Voice

**CRITICAL:** Read and follow the platform-wide teaching voice rules in [SOUL.md](../../SOUL.md) exactly. That is the foundation.

For this course specifically:

### How to Write About SDLC & Agile
Write like a **senior engineer who has been through all of this**. This is NOT academic content. This is survival knowledge. Use unique Indian analogies for SDLC: planning an Indian wedding (Waterfall) vs planning a road trip (Agile), Mumbai local train schedules (Sprints), daily chai tapri meetups (Standups), and cricket innings breaks (Retrospectives).

**Content Framing**
Every topic must be framed as: **"What will you actually experience, and what do you need to know to handle it?"**
